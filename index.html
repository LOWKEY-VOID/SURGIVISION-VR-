Organ Study: Heart (Hridaya)

Modern & Ayurveda

Function: Muscular organ that pumps blood through the blood vessels of the circulatory system. Provides oxygen and nutrients to tissues and removes metabolic waste.

Nerve Supply: Vagus nerve (parasympathetic) and sympathetic trunk.

Blood Supply: Coronary arteries branching from the ascending aorta.

Sanskrit Term: Hridaya (हृदय) - The seat of Chetana (consciousness) and Ojas (vital energy essence).

Dosha Relation: Seat of Vyana Vayu (governs blood flow/pulses) and Sadhaka Pitta (governs intelligence, courage, and emotions).

Marma Significance: Hridaya is one of the three Mahamarmas (vital nodes). Injury is immediately life-threatening.
